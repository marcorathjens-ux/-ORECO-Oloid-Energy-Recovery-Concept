# Project-ORECO (Oloid Energy Recovery Concept)

## Overview
ORECO is an innovative energy harvesting system designed for Electric Vehicle (EV) cooling circuits. By utilizing the unique geometry of the **Oloid**, the system converts hydraulic flow and thermal energy into electrical power without mechanical wear.
