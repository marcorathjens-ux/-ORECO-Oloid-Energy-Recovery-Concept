# ELECTRONICS_MEMU.md
**Micro-Energy-Management-Unit (MEMU)** – *High-Efficiency Power Conditioning for ORECO Systems.*

## 1. Functional Overview
The MEMU serves as the essential bridge between the irregular AC voltage generated by the ORECO induction coils and the stable DC output required for automotive-grade sensors (temperature, pressure, flow) or wireless communication modules (BLE/NFC).

## 2. Circuit Architecture

### A. Active Rectification (Input Stage)
Since the oloid-driven induction generates power in the milliwatt range, traditional diode bridges would suffer from excessive voltage drops.
*   **Design:** Low-Forward-Voltage Schottky Matrix.
*   **Objective:** Maximize energy harvest during low-flow (startup) conditions.

### B. Energy Buffering (Storage Stage)
To compensate for flow fluctuations and torque ripples during the oloid’s precession.
*   **Component:** Ultra-Low Leakage Supercapacitor.
*   **Capacity:** 0.1F to 0.47F (depending on the load profile of the target sensor).

### C. DC/DC Conversion (Voltage Regulation)
The core utilizes a specialized "cold-start" harvester IC capable of extracting energy from extremely low input voltages.
*   **Primary IC:** **LTC3108** (Ultralow Voltage Step-Up Converter).
*   **Cold-Start Threshold:** Functional from as low as **20mV** input.

## 3. Technical Specifications


| Parameter | Target Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Input Voltage (AC)** | 20mV - 5.0V (Frequency dependent) |
| **Output Voltage (DC)** | 3.3V (Regulated) |
| **Quiescent Current** | < 1µA (Ideal for idle battery monitoring) |
| **Max Efficiency** | > 85% at optimized flow rates |
| **Operating Temp** | -40°C to +125°C (Automotive Grade) |

## 5. EMI & Integration Strategy
*   **Shielding:** The MEMU is housed in a compact, shielded aluminum enclosure integrated directly into the ORECO flange.
*   **Isolation:** Ferrite beads and bypass capacitors are used on the DC output to prevent electromagnetic interference (EMI) with the vehicle's internal communication buses (CAN/LIN).
*   **Scalability:** The low component count allows for a unit cost < $2.00 at high-volume production (OEM scale).
