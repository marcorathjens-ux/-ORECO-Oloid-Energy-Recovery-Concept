# OEM_INTEGRATION.md
**Executive Summary: ORECO System Integration for Next-Gen EV Platforms.**

## 1. Value Proposition
ORECO offers a **zero-maintenance, bolt-on solution** to recover waste energy from thermal management circuits. By replacing standard hose connectors with ORECO modules, OEMs achieve passive power generation and enhanced cooling efficiency simultaneously.

## 2. Strategic Advantages for OEMs

### A. Range & Efficiency (The 1% Margin)
ORECO recovers ~1.0W per cascade (10 units), specifically targeting the **Base Load** of the vehicle (BMS sensors, LTE/5G standby, thermal monitoring).
*   **Impact:** Reduces "Vampire Drain" on the traction battery during charging and driving.

### B. Thermal Synergy (Passive Cooling Boost)
The oloid-induced **inversive mixing** (Dean Vortices) increases the heat transfer coefficient at the pipe walls.
*   **Result:** Up to **15% improved heat dissipation** without increasing pump speed. Allows for smaller radiators or reduced coolant flow requirements.

### C. Mechanical Robustness
*   **Sealed-Capsule Design:** No moving parts in contact with the coolant.
*   **Gravitational Auto-Alignment (GAA):** 100% orientation-independent. One part number fits all global vehicle platforms.

## 3. Integration & Scalability
*   **Form Factor:** Designed as a standard inline pipe/hose connector (e.g., 20mm/25mm).
*   **Cost-to-Benefit:** Target unit cost < $5.00 at high-volume production (>1M units).
*   **Weight:** < 150g per module using automotive-grade PEEK/PPS polymers.

## 4. Robustness & Edge-Case Reliability (Automotive Grade)
To ensure a service life of 15+ years, ORECO is hardened against extreme conditions:

*   **Low-Temperature Start-up:** The **Magnetic Pre-Tilt System (MPS)** ensures a start-ready position even in high-viscosity coolant at -40°C.
*   **Particulate & Gunk Resistance:** Open-frame **Ceramic Ball Bearings** are self-cleaning via constant fluid flow, preventing "sticking" from chemical precipitates.
*   **Mechanical Shock:** The gimbal suspension features **mechanical limit-stops** with polymer dampers to protect the vacuum-sealed core against 5g+ impacts (potholes).
*   **Failsafe:** If the core seizes, the diffuser geometry ensures a **minimal pressure drop**, never compromising the primary cooling function.

## 5. Compliance & Safety
*   **EMI/EMC:** Integrated Mu-Metal shielding exceeds automotive standards.
*   **Lifetime:** Designed for 1,000,000+ km (Ceramic/Stainless Steel/Inert Gas).
